Section 01

Platform Decision: Astro — Not WordPress

Verdict

Astro + Vercel. Static generation. Zero cost.

matrixdestiny.co is a programmatic SEO site at its core — hundreds of arcana pages, compatibility combinations, karmic intersections across three languages. Astro generates all of these as pure static HTML at build time. Pages load instantly from Vercel's CDN. No database queries. No plugins. No monthly hosting cost. Same approach as 5letterwordvault — just a bigger content structure.

Why not WordPress: Database-driven. Every programmatic page hits the database on load. At 490+ pages across three languages it gets slow and expensive. Your competitor uses WordPress and is losing 43K traffic per month. You serve static HTML from a global CDN in milliseconds.

Section 06

Koray Semantic Framework — Applied

The Koray Tuğberk Gübür framework (TMAP) builds topical authority through entity-based semantic SEO. Every content decision maps to a semantic signal Google's NLP layer reads.

Rule 01 — Entity-First Architecture

Every page targets an entity, not just a keyword. Google processes pages through entity recognition (BERT/MUM). Your 22 arcana pages, 10 energy zone pages, and all calculator pages are entity pages — they tell Google your site is the authoritative source for Matrix of Destiny entities.

Primary entity: "Matrix of Destiny" → Attribute entities: [arcana, energy zones, compatibility, predestination, numerology] → Sub-entities: [Arcana 1 … Arcana 22] → Context: [birth date, Tarot, Natalia Ladini, karma]

Rule 02 — Query-State First (Not Keyword First)

Every page must resolve a user emotional or life state — not just target a keyword phrase. This changes page titles, H1s, and content direction entirely.

Bad: "arcana 18" · Good: "arcana 18 emotional confusion meaning" · Good: "arcana 18 relationship fears" · Good: "why arcana 18 causes emotional uncertainty"

Rule 03 — Context Vectors

Each article must contain the right surrounding entities for Google to place it in the correct semantic neighbourhood. A cluster article about "financial zone" must mention arcana numbers, energy flow, and matrix calculation — not just the keyword phrase.

Article: "Financial Zone in Matrix of Destiny" → Required context: [arcana 10, arcana 6, karmic programmes, birth date calculation, money block, energy zone map] → Forbidden context (dilutes signal): [general finance tips, budgeting advice]

Rule 04 — Entity Relationship Tables (Mandatory on Every Entity Page)

Every arcana page, energy zone page, and pillar article must include a structured relationship table. This is what Google's knowledge graph reads — not just prose.

IS-A: Arcana 18 IS-A moon archetype — emotional intuition and subconscious fear
USED-FOR: identifying emotional confusion, fear-driven decisions, intuition blockages
WORKS-WITH: Arcana 2 (inner knowing), Arcana 7 (self-control)
CONFLICTS-WITH: emotional suppression, rigid control energy
CAUSES: anxiety cycles, relationship confusion, avoidance patterns

Rule 05 — All Four Query Types Per Cluster

Covering all four query types signals complete topical coverage. Missing one type leaves a semantic gap competitors can exploit.

INFORMATIONAL: "What is Matrix of Destiny?" / "What does arcana 7 mean?"
NAVIGATIONAL: "Matrix of Destiny calculator" / "free matrix reading online"
COMMERCIAL INVESTIGATION: "Matrix of Destiny vs numerology" / "best matrix reading service"
TRANSACTIONAL: "buy matrix of destiny reading" / "matrix of destiny consultant online"

Rule 06 — Internal Linking Architecture (Non-Negotiable)

Every article links to its cluster pillar. Every pillar links to all supporting articles. Every arcana entity page links to every cluster article that mentions it. Every page routes back to the calculator. No orphan pages.

Arcana 9 page → links to: Cluster 1 pillar, Cluster 5 (life purpose), Cluster 10 (personal development), all articles mentioning Arcana 9, personal calculator
Links from: all articles that reference Arcana 9 → back to this entity page

Rule 07 — AI Retrieval Engineering (Every Major Page)

Every major page must be extraction-ready for Google AI Overviews, ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude. Your competitor is losing ChatGPT citations. You capture them by being more structured.

Required on every major page: concise definition in first paragraph · extraction-ready summary block · semantic relationship table · FAQ block with 5–8 questions · "best for" statement · Article + FAQPage + BreadcrumbList schema · semantic H2 headings covering all entity attributes
