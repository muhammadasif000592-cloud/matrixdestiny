This is a breaking change from the current PT-BR and Ukrainian files, which use the Thoth/Marseilles order (8 = Justice, 11 = Strength). All files must be renumbered and content-audited before the English set is published.

## Notes on Arcana 8, 11, and 14 Content Boundaries

**Arcana 8 — Strength** governs: the psychological relationship with power and authority; dominance-submission patterns; capacity to delegate without anxiety; calibrated self-assertion; the control-vs-surrender tension. Domain keyword: *power regulation*.

**Arcana 11 — Justice** governs: cause-and-effect recognition; calibrated accountability; proportionality; pattern repetition from denied contribution or over-responsibility; relational reciprocity; the victim-vs-over-responsibility tension. Domain keyword: *consequence recognition*.

**Arcana 14 — Temperance** governs: emotional regulation; nervous-system balance; intensity management; explosion vs suppression cycling; the window of tolerance; somatic processing of emotional states; recovery from high-activation states. Domain keyword: *intensity metabolism*.

These are three distinct domains. Do not conflate them in content, FAQ questions, or cross-references.

**Arcana 8 — Strength** does NOT govern: cause-and-effect patterns, emotional regulation, or nervous-system intensity. Those belong to arcana 11 and 14 respectively.

**Arcana 11 — Justice** does NOT govern: emotional regulation, emotional intensity, impulse management, or the window of tolerance. Those belong to arcana 14 (Temperance).

**Arcana 14 — Temperance** does NOT govern: power dynamics or authority (arcana 8), or cause-and-effect accountability (arcana 11).

The PT-BR and Ukrainian arcana 11 files currently contain emotional regulation content that belongs to arcana 14 (Temperance). That content has been preserved in `arcana-14-temperance-content.md`. The arcana 11 files in all three languages must be rewritten as Justice content (consequence recognition, proportional accountability, relational reciprocity). See misalignment audit for the full fix sequence.
