# Content Quality Rules — matrixdestiny.co

## Word counts
- Arcana entity pages: 1,500 minimum, 2,000 target
- Pillar HUB articles: 2,500 minimum, 3,500 target
- Emotional state articles: 1,200 minimum, 1,800 target
- FAQ and terminal pages: 800 minimum

## Zero tolerance
- No mystical fluff
- No vague spiritual language
- No filler phrases: "it is important to note", "in conclusion", "this powerful archetype", "deeply transformative", "cosmic energy", "universal vibration"
- No generic polarity: never "positive" or "negative" without behavioral mechanism
- No thin FAQ answers: minimum 4 sentences each
- No repetition across sections

## Required on every arcana page
- Specific behavioral mechanism for every claim
- Concrete psychological scenarios: conflict avoidance, emotional withdrawal, control struggles, dependency loops, idealization patterns
- Active vs blocked section with 4 behavioral examples each
- Three life domains: professional, relationships, karmic pattern
- Entity relationship table: IS-A, USED-FOR, WORKS-WITH, CONFLICTS-WITH, CAUSES
- FAQ block: 5 questions minimum

## Review process
- Build one page as sample
- Stop and wait for approval
- Do not build remaining pages until sample is approved
- Do not commit or push until approval given
